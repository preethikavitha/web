About Us

The Government College of Technology (GCT) is a premier engineering institution in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1945, GCT has a rich history of providing quality technical education and producing skilled professionals who contribute to various industries and sectors globally.With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a commitment to excellence, GCT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, and applied sciences.At GCT, we strive to foster innovation, creativity, and leadership among our students, preparing them to tackle real-world challenges and make meaningful contributions to society.
